Our Guiding Principles

Mission: Promoting Spiritual Healing

Our mission at COPS is to foster spiritual healing within the community. We achieve this by wholeheartedly embracing, actively supporting, and truly empowering individuals and their families who grapple with the challenges of addiction-related problems, equipping them with essential basic life skills training.

Vision: Cultivating Spiritual and Productive Lives

COPS envisions a future where individuals affected by addiction become spiritually grounded and productive members of their community. We strive to see them successfully raising their families, securing meaningful employment, and ensuring access to quality education for themselves and their loved ones. We recognize the challenges faced by children growing up without a father figure due to incarceration, drugs, and other adverse circumstances, and our work is dedicated to fostering positive change.

"Restoring hope, transforming lives"

OUR PURPOSE

The COPS Mission

Founded in 2002 as a faith-based organization, COPS incorporates spirituality into our certified outpatient Substance Use Disorder (SUD) treatment program to help individuals achieve stable recovery and become productive members of their families and communities.

Spiritual Foundation

We believe spirituality is essential to lasting recovery and personal transformation.

Community Focus

Helping individuals reintegrate as productive family and community members.

Holistic Approach

Addressing all aspects of life through our transitional housing and support services.
